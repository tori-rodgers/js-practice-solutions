How to Launch the Debugger / DevTools

Reminder: For breakpoints, debugger statements, and some errors to appear, you must have DevTools open before refreshing the page.
  • Google Chrome / Microsoft Edge:
    • Windows/Linux: Press F12 or Ctrl + Shift + I
    • Mac: Press Cmd + Option + I
  • Mozilla Firefox:
    • Windows/Linux: Press F12 or Ctrl + Shift + I
    • Mac: Press Cmd + Option + I
  • Safari:
    • Mac: Enable the Develop menu in Safari Preferences (Advanced tab), then press Cmd + Option + I

You can also right-click anywhere on the page and select Inspect or Inspect Element in most browsers.
